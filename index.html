About The Event

Join us for an unforgettable night with Baskara Hindia, one of Indonesia's most talented musicians, at our annual school festival. This exclusive performance will feature hits from his acclaimed albums along with special surprises.

The event will take place at our school's amphitheater, transformed into an intimate concert venue with state-of-the-art sound and lighting systems.
